GSASET2023

Applied-Science-2023 provides a wonderful platform in bringing together leading scientists, research scholars, research students, practitioners, engineers, academic professionals and policy makers with highly cited plenary lectures, keynote lectures and invited talks to share their latest innovations in the fields of Applied Science, Engineering and technology.

In addition, variety of Young research Forums, poster forum along with workshops, special sessions, contributed papers, video and oral poster presentations covering all frontier topics on latest research progress and innovations would be interested in audience. We make sure to support and motivate Young Researchers and Students “3 Best Young Researcher Award” and “3 Best Poster Award” at Applied-Science-2023 Platform.
