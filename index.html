(GSASET)2023

The three days of the conference will contribute to build long-term profitable and sustainable communication among the participants in a wide variety of scientific areas with the common interest in Applied Science, Engineering and Technology.

2023.03.23(TUE) ~ 25(WED)

@SHAGRI-LA HOTEL, BANGKOK, THAILAND

Participation Opportunities

  • Oral Presentaion

    Oral Presentation is an effective way to present your valuable research work to get recognized. Oral Presentations consist of Plenary, Keynote and a series of Invited Talks with specific time durations.

  • Poster Presentation

    A poster presentation is another potential channel to disseminate your research of a topic in a short and concise format. Posters offer the opportunity to engage with other conference attendees interested.

  • Workshop Presention

    Workshop provides a high-yield, more efficient and effective tool for discussion, problem solving and feedback about one’s scholarly work. Each workshop lasts for 1 hour intensive and interactive discussion.
